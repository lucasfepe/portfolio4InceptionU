About Me

Lucas Ferrari Pereira is a multidisciplinary creator and problem solver with a unique blend of expertise in software development, game design, chemical engineering, and dance. His journey began with a BASc in Chemical Engineering from the University of Toronto, where he honed his analytical and problem-solving skills.

His curiosity for technology led him to the world of software and game development. Lucas has worked as a QA engineer and software developer, gaining experience in building and testing applications while continuously expanding his technical skill set. His passion for programming ignited when he discovered the power of automation, streamlining processes and optimizing efficiency.

This drive pushed him to develop projects such as Celestes, an online multiplayer collectible card game, showcasing his expertise in Unity, AWS, and cloud-based solutions.

Beyond tech, Lucas has a deep appreciation for the arts. His involvement with Xing Dance Theatre and Decidedly Jazz Danceworks highlights his dedication to movement and creative expression, balancing the logical rigor of coding with the fluidity of dance.

With a strong foundation in engineering, an ever-growing passion for software development, and a creative mindset shaped by dance, Lucas thrives at the intersection of logic and artistry. Whether building scalable applications, designing engaging game experiences, or expressing himself through movement, he brings innovation, precision, and energy to everything he does.
